# Landa Case Management System - Futuristic Design Document

## Design Philosophy - Next Generation Landa Experience

### Color Palette (Futuristic High-Tech)
- **Deep Space Black**: #000000 - Pure black for ultimate contrast
- **Quantum Black**: #0a0a0a - Primary background with depth
- **Landa Electric Blue**: #0066cc - Primary brand color (exact Landa blue)
- **Cyber Blue**: #00d4ff - Secondary accent for highlights and interactions
- **Quantum Silver**: #c0c0c0 - Metallic accents and text
- **Plasma White**: #ffffff - High contrast elements
- **Neon Cyan**: #00ffff - Accent color for AI elements and special features

### Typography (Futuristic & Professional)
- **Display Font**: "Orbitron" - Ultra-modern, geometric for headings and brand elements
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - Clean, highly legible for content and UI elements
- **Technical Font**: "JetBrains Mono" - Monospace for code, IDs, and technical data
- **Logo Font**: Custom geometric sans-serif for "LANDA" branding

### Visual Language - "Digital Printing Revolution"
- **Theme**: Next-generation digital printing meets AI and automation
- **Aesthetic**: Ultra-modern, minimalist, high-contrast with subtle futuristic elements
- **Mood**: Cutting-edge, professional, trustworthy, innovative, premium
- **Inspiration**: Landa's nanography technology, quantum computing, space-age interfaces
